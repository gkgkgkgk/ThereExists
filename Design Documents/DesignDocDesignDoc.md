# The Design Document Design Document

## Purpose
This document outlines the design process for the game "There Exists".

## Design Document Types
There are three types of design documents:
1. **Game Design Documents**: These documents outline the overall design of the game, and begine with "TE_"
2. **Technical Design Documents**: These documents outline the technical implementation of specific features, and begine with "TD_"
3. **Phase Design Documents**: These documents outline the design of specific phases, and belong in a parent directory called "Phases" in a sub-directory named with the date of the design document.
