# Spacecraft Operating System
This document describes the Spacecraft Operating System. The OS runs the ship, and the player can interact with it through a variety of controls, but primarily through the OS interface.

## Functionality
The OS can interface with sensors, power systems, flight systems, and probe systems. It can also interface with the ship's AI, which can help the player with a variety of tasks.


## Interaction Methods
From the OS interface, the player can do a number of things.

### The CLI
The CLI is a command-line interface that allows the player to interact with the ship's systems. With the CLI, the user can control anything on their ship. While it may not be the most efficient way to control the ship, it is the most powerful. The CLI will be required for important decisions that require a human in the loop that the AI cannot make on its own, such as cutting power, initiating emergency protocols, or executing complex maneuvers. 

### AI Co-Pilot
The AI Co-Pilot can help the player with a variety of tasks, such as:
* Summarizing sensor data
* Suggesting courses of action
* Managing ship systems
* Explaining complex concepts
